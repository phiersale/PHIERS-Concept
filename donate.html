How Your Donation Supports Healthcare, Jobs, Economic Relief, Democracy, and Peace

PHIERS uses every dollar to strengthen nonpartisan civic engagement across all five dimensions of the 5D Solutions framework: Healthcare, Jobs, Economic Stability, Democratic Power, and Peace. We don’t fund candidates or parties. We fund the tools and organizing that help working people, union members, and everyday Americans push back against concentrated power and say: No kings. No unchecked authority. No decisions made without us.

  • Healthcare: Public education and organizing around the $600 universal telehealth plan — the first domino that unlocks the entire 5D cascade.
  • Jobs: Outreach and materials explaining how the healthcare savings fund 8–14 million new AI‑resistant, community‑based jobs that strengthen working families.
  • Economic Relief: Tools that show how the $2.73T annual savings create room for monthly assistance, lower costs of living, and a real path to stability.
  • Democracy: District‑level organizing that helps constituents coordinate, speak up, and apply pressure — the math‑based strategy that restores representation.
  • Peace: Nonpartisan civic engagement that reduces authoritarian overreach by empowering people to demand accountability instead of accepting top‑down rule.
  • Worker‑Centered Outreach: Partnerships with labor voices and community leaders who fight for dignity, fairness, and shared power.
  • Digital Tools for Collective Action: Mobile‑friendly systems that help people contact their representatives, track accountability, and organize locally.
  • Volunteer Coordination: Training and infrastructure for people who want to help their communities push back against concentrated power.

Every dollar strengthens the movement of Americans who refuse to be ruled — people who believe in healthcare, good jobs, economic stability, real democracy, and peace.
