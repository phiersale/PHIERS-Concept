Why Medicaid Comes First

Reform usually fails because it tries to cover everyone at once. PHIERS starts where savings are largest and people are most vulnerable.

  • Highest cost per person = highest savings
  • Protects low-income, elderly, and disabled first
  • Creates proof at massive scale

Once Medicaid works, expansion to ACA, private insurance, and the uninsured becomes inevitable.
