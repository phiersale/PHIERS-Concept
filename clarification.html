The History: Eliminating Healthcare's Profit Motive

Al Wilson and Bill Wiggins founded SureSafe Pharma (2007), pioneering the not-for-profit medication access model that imports medications WITHOUT markups or middlemen. This model proved that eliminating profit from pharmaceutical distribution works at scale.

The Strategy: SureSafe imported medications without middlemen. PHIERS extends this by funneling cooperative members to undercut the market through volume, passing savings directly to CMS to build toward Medicare for All with price controls - not profit.

Proof of Concept: Mark Cuban independently validated this exact approach when he launched Cost Plus Drugs in 2022 - 15 years after SureSafe demonstrated it worked. 7 million customers. 80-90% savings. No middlemen.

Over 16 years, we've pioneered these concepts and published them freely. Now multiple sources prove they work at scale - including ZORTT Elite Medical's telemedicine marketplace and community-enabling model, and other new platforms offering connected community experiences.
