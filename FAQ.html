1 minute 11 seconds. This is why they're terrified.

1. Is this realistic?

+

Yes — because it's funded by math, not hope.

This isn't a wishlist. It's a financially grounded systems upgrade.

+

Because it's the first domino — and the one that pays for the rest.

Telehealth is already:

  • Cheaper
  • Faster
  • Nationwide
  • Scalable
  • Proven

And the savings from TeleCARE cover the cost of every other PHIERS benefit after the first year of operation.

5. Is this partisan?

+

No.

Healthcare, stability, jobs, and democracy are not partisan issues — they're human issues.

The only requirement is wanting a country that works.

6. Is this socialism?

+

No.

PHIERS is a public‑benefit corporation and a grassroots cooperative marketplace and insurance plan — 100% owned, operated, and accountable to its members (We the People).

Nothing is nationalized.
Nothing is government‑run.
Nothing is controlled by donors or corporations that profit from keeping people sick, poor, or divided.

This is market‑efficient, cost‑reducing, and freedom‑expanding.

7. How does this get funded?

+

By saving money — not spending more.

TeleCARE offered through the ACA exchange becomes a lower‑cost option for CMS, allowing them to save money directly attributable to awarding contracts to the people's platform.

PHIERS can also receive performance‑based grants because it undercuts every competitor while improving outcomes.

No new taxes.
No new bureaucracy.
Just redirecting waste into solutions that work.

8. How does this create jobs?

+

By creating a stable, inclusive, community‑powered economy.

As a cooperative and bartering platform, PHIERS supports work from:

  • Health and social workers
  • Mental health professionals
  • Medical transportation providers
  • Addiction, trauma, and depression counselors
  • Child and adult day‑care providers
  • Tradespeople for repairs and maintenance
  • Community gardeners, cooks, cleaners, and builders
  • Anyone providing value to their community

Instead of paying only in dollars, PHIERS provides benefits through the PHIERsale marketplace and the PHIERplace resource‑matching network.

This creates self‑sustaining, meaningful jobs that improve life for everyone — both the beneficiary and the provider.

9. How does this protect democracy?

+

By restoring accountability.

When we reach 1,500 signatures on the petition, representatives are compelled to attend town halls where constituents present clear, district‑specific demands.

This is how we force Congress to:

  • End the wars
  • Prevent the draft
  • Authorize TeleCARE through the ACA
  • Unlock trillions in annual savings
  • Put constituent interests ahead of the donors

If they refuse, we recall, replace, or primary every candidate who ignores their constituents.

This is how democracy protects itself.

10. What happens after I sign?

Three things:

  1. Your signature counts toward your district's 1,500 petition signature threshold.
  2. Your representative becomes accountable to your district's demands.
  3. You're invited to town hall meetings and ongoing surveys to keep your voice front and center.

Once petition, marketplace and benefit thresholds are reached, you'll be notified and invited to participate as each component comes online.

We expect major milestones before July 4th — because that's when we plan to reclaim our independence from authoritarian drift and a do‑nothing Congress.
